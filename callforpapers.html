Call for Papers

Original Papers, Case Studies, review articles, application-oriented research from researchers which is not currently under review by another conference or journal and addressing state-of-the-art Innovation and Research are invited to submit their work to the conference.

Paper Submission Guidelines

General Information

All submissions must be written in English. All papers must be original unpublished work. All papers must be submitted electronically by the deadline. A person should not have more than two papers as the first author. The full paper must be presented at the conference by at least one of the listed co-authors in order to be included in the conference proceedings. Sources of financial support, if any, must be clearly disclosed. If abbreviations are used in the text, they should be defined in the text at first use. All pages of manuscript including Tables and Figures should be numbered.

Format of Paper

Full paper not more than 8 pages (Maximum 5000 words) including abstract, figures, tables, charts, references etc as prescribed in publication* template. At most two extra pages can be included as per the instructions provided under registration tab.

*The organizing committee will soon share the prescribed format after acceptance of Publication House (Elsevier, AIP, IEEE etc) for proceedings.

Participation opportunities in hybrid mode

If not able to attend the conference in person and wanted to share your research with international community. The participant can join as a virtual presenter. The joining credentials through meeting link will be shared at the time of respective session of the track.

Publications Opportunities

FEST 2023 inviting original and high quality, unpublished research papers addressing the recent research challenges, developments, research outcomes, experimental results and future directions in the fields matching to the theme of conference. All the submissions will go through the plagiarism check and double-blind, peer review by at least three reviewers. Only accepted, registered & presented papers of the Conference by authors, will be submitted for review and publication. Authors are requested to kindly refrain from plagiarism in any form. Authors should submit their original and unpublished research work not under consideration for publication elsewhere. Papers found to be plagiarized and parallel submissions shall be rejected during any stage of review.

  • Papers submitted and presented at the conference will be published in the conference proceedings, subject to their acceptance by the review committee, immediately after the conference, which will be available to view and download.
  • The publication opportunities will be updated.Each conference proceedings will have a unique digit International Standard Serial Number (ISSN) and/or International Standard Book Number (ISBN) code.
  • *Extended versions of selected papers will be submitted in Special Issue in International Journal of Computer & Electrical Engineering, Elsevier (SCI) and some as Book Chapters in Edited book with CRC Press and Taylor & Francis (Scopus)

*We are working with internationally accredited journals from renowned publisher like Elsevier, AIP, IEEE etc. The website bill be updated once we receive acceptance.

ABES Institute of Technology, embarked on its mission in the field of Technical Education in the year 2007. It is situated on a prominent location at National Highway-09, that is approximately 19 km east of New Delhi. It is approved by AICTE and affiliated to Dr. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. The institute offers Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) programs in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE), Information Technology (IT), Computer Science & Engineering in Artificial Intelligence (AI), Data Science (DS), Internet of Things (IoT) and Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE). B.Tech. in CSE & IT are accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA) New Delhi. Institute also offers Master of Computer Applications (MCA) degree course.
